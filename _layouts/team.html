Biography

Dr. Yan Wang is an Associated Professor with the Department of Computer and Information Sciences at Temple University. Currently, his research interests lie on Mobile Computing, Smart Healthcare, Internet of Things and Deep Learning. He also regularly serves as Technical Program Committee and Organizing Committee in INFOCOM, CNS, ICDCS and ICCCN. His research has been reported in numerous media outlets including MIT Technology Review, CNN, Fox News Channel, Wall Street Journal, National Public Radio, and IEEE Spectrum.
